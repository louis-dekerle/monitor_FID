Dear reader,

In the repository you will find the scripts and data used in the paper To flee or not to flee? Spatial differences in FID of water monitors (Varanus salvator) across an urban matrix.
\nThe scripts are dry_season.Rmd replicate the dry season results and rainy_season.Rmd to replicate the rainy season results. 
\nThe data used are in the DATA/pro folder. data_obs8.csv is the dataset for the dry season (I may have to rename it now that I think of it) and fid_monitor_rainyss_process.csv is the one for the rainy season. (data_all merge both of them and wasn't used in the paper)
\nThe archive folders contain ancient knowledge of old scripts and obsolete datasets used during the study.
